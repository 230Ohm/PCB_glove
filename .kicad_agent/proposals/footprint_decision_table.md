Status definitions:

- **Verified**: exact MPN and footprint checked against an authoritative local drawing. No item currently meets this threshold.
- **Provisional**: plausible standard library footprint assigned for drafting but still needs document and manufacturing review.
- **Placeholder**: deliberately generic or project-local DRAFT_ preparation footprint; not approved for layout.
- **Blocked**: no footprint assigned because the exact part or documentation is missing.

## Assignment policy

Unknown DK, finger, power, regulator, protection, and camera items deliberately have no exact footprint assigned. The two local footprints are preparation aids only; both begin with DRAFT_ and contain visible DRAFT / VERIFY BEFORE LAYOUT text on fabrication and silkscreen layers. No footprint has been placed on PCB_glove/PCB_glove.kicad_pcb.
