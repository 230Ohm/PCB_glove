# Gate P - physical qualification before fabrication

Every item below is open and carries the exact classification:

`DEFERRED PHYSICAL VALIDATION — NOT MEASURED`

- actual STM32N6570-DK identity, revision and connector condition;
- actual solder-bridge, BOOT switch, option-byte, OTP/lifecycle and debug-authentication state;
- actual firmware image identity and reset/fault state behavior;
- all five CS voltages during off, reset, boot, halt, disconnect and asymmetric-power states;
- CS rise/decay, pull-up current, leakage and backfeed current;
- SCK, MOSI, MISO and INT waveforms and logic levels;
- SPI mode 3 operation and signal integrity at the intended cable length and final clock;
- connector continuity, pin-1 orientation, polarity and DNC verification on assembled hardware;
- four independent breakout trial-mate, retention, removal clearance and no-common-rigid-plane proof;
- Molex housing/terminal/crimp/wire assembly and pull-strength verification;
- ground offset, ground-return impedance and cable voltage drop;
- reverse-current and DK-to-glove/glove-to-DK backfeed tests;
- power-path no-load, load, fault, current-limit, ripple, accuracy and shutdown tests;
- diode drop, regulator temperature, local hot spots and skin-contact thermal review;
- strain relief, bend envelope, snag, retention, service access and probe access;
- wearable contact, motion, sweat/environmental and user-safety qualification.

No fabrication output, purchasing release or wearable-use claim is authorized until Gate P is completed and reviewed.
